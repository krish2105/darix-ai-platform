TARGET AUDIENCE
Dubai businesses, SMEs, startup founders, enterprise leaders, AI consultants, recruiters, investors, university professors, and digital transformation teams.

WEBSITE GOAL
Create a premium, professional, fully responsive, 3D animated SaaS website that helps businesses assess their AI readiness, calculate a score, view dashboard insights, and get an AI adoption roadmap.

CORE PRODUCT IDEA
DARIX AI helps organizations measure AI readiness across strategy, data maturity, technology, automation, people, governance, use cases, and ROI. Users complete an assessment and receive a readiness score, maturity level, recommendations, and a 30/60/90-day AI transformation roadmap.
